Angela's Recipe for the Best
Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 3/2 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons ground Cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instructors

  1. Mix the mmilk with yeast,sugar,eggs.
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture.
  3. Add in the floor and mix until combined into a dough.
  4. knead the dough for 10 minutes.
  5. Transfer the dough into a laege bowl and cover with plastic wrap leave
    it somewhere to rise for 2 hours.
